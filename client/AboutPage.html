About us

KMV Canteen is a web project designed by using basic html, javascript and node js. This product provides an easy way to order food at your college canteen without waiting in lines without any service charges. This product eases food ordering for college students and staff since you don't have wait in lines or worry for cash to buy food. This product has been developed by us for educational/learning purpose and is still in its development phase thus your feedback is really valuable for us. We are working really hard everyday to bring new features for you.
